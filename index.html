Note: Academic Dishonesty/Misconduct or failing to maintain Academic Integrity in a Dental Institute includes cheating on examinations, plagiarizing assignments, disobeying professors by engaging in forbidden activities, forging or fabricating patient data, lying to faculty members to gain unfair advantage, lying to patients, and falsifying institutional documents (Turner SP, Beemsterboer PL. Enhancing academic integrity: formulating effective honor codes. J Dent Educ. 2003;67(10):1122-1129.).
